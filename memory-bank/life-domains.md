# Life Domains — LENSSTACK + X10THINK

## Domain Philosophy
Life is not siloed. Domains are **lenses** through which you view different dimensions of your one integrated life. Progress in one domain affects others. The system tracks this interdependence.

## The 12 Domains

### 1. Health
**Scope**: Physical fitness, sleep, nutrition, preventive care, energy management
**Vision template**: "In 10 years, my health will allow me to..."
**Key metrics**: Sleep quality, exercise frequency, energy levels
**Default rituals**: Morning exercise, sleep schedule, nutrition tracking

### 2. Career
**Scope**: Professional role, skills development, impact, income, reputation
**Vision template**: "In my career, I am known for..."
**Key metrics**: Skill progression, career milestone dates, income trajectory
**Default rituals**: Weekly skill practice, deep work sessions, networking

### 3. Financial
**Scope**: Income, savings rate, investments, net worth, financial independence
**Vision template**: "My financial life enables me to..."
**Key metrics**: Savings rate, investment growth, financial goals
**Default rituals**: Monthly financial review, investment check-in

### 4. Intellectual
**Scope**: Learning, reading, mental growth, curiosity, mastery
**Vision template**: "My intellectual life is defined by..."
**Key metrics**: Books read, courses completed, concepts mastered
**Default rituals**: Daily reading, weekly learning session, note synthesis

### 5. Emotional
**Scope**: Emotional intelligence, self-awareness, mental health, resilience, therapy
**Vision template**: "Emotionally, I am someone who..."
**Key metrics**: Mood trends, reflection frequency, emotional vocabulary
**Default rituals**: Journaling, meditation, therapy sessions

### 6. Spiritual
**Scope**: Values, meaning, purpose, connection to something greater, inner life
**Vision template**: "My spiritual life is grounded in..."
**Key metrics**: Alignment with values, sense of purpose (subjective)
**Default rituals**: Contemplation, values reflection, gratitude practice

### 7. Relationships
**Scope**: Romantic partner, deep friendships (5–10 people), quality of connection
**Vision template**: "The relationships I most cherish are..."
**Key metrics**: Quality time with key people, relationship depth
**Default rituals**: Weekly intentional time with partner, monthly deep conversations

### 8. Family
**Scope**: Parents, siblings, children, extended family, family culture
**Vision template**: "As a family member, I show up by..."
**Key metrics**: Quality time, family traditions, presence
**Default rituals**: Family time blocks, parent calls, family rituals

### 9. Social
**Scope**: Broader social network, community, professional network, giving back
**Vision template**: "In my social world, I contribute..."
**Key metrics**: Community involvement, social impact, network health
**Default rituals**: Community activities, networking events

### 10. Character
**Scope**: Integrity, virtues, principles, identity, values in action
**Vision template**: "The character traits I am building are..."
**Key metrics**: Alignment between stated values and actions
**Default rituals**: Values reflection, character goals review

### 11. Lifestyle
**Scope**: Home environment, hobbies, aesthetics, travel, possessions, daily quality of life
**Vision template**: "My lifestyle supports my best life by..."
**Key metrics**: Home environment quality, hobby engagement, travel goals
**Default rituals**: Home maintenance, hobby time, environment optimization

### 12. Legacy
**Scope**: Long-term contribution, what you leave behind, how you'll be remembered, impact on world
**Vision template**: "My legacy will be..."
**Key metrics**: Impact projects, content created, people mentored
**Default rituals**: Legacy project work, mentoring, contribution tracking

## Domain Customization
- Users can rename domains
- Add custom domains (max 16 total)
- Hide inactive domains
- Reorder domains
- Custom icons and colors

## Domain Health Score
```
health_score = (
  goal_completion_rate * 0.35 +
  execution_rate * 0.35 +
  reflection_frequency * 0.20 +
  user_manual_rating * 0.10
) * 100
```

## Balance Wheel
- Radar chart showing all 12 domain scores
- Target: balanced high scores across all domains
- AI detects: domains neglected for >30 days, severe imbalances
- Not a shame tool — a strategic awareness tool

## Domain Interdependence Map
Some domain relationships:
- Health ↔ Career (energy enables performance)
- Intellectual ↔ Career (skills drive advancement)
- Relationships ↔ Emotional (connection requires emotional capacity)
- Financial ↔ Lifestyle (resources enable choices)
- Character ↔ Legacy (who you are determines what you leave)

AI surfaces these correlations when relevant.
