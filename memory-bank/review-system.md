# Review System — LENSSTACK + X10THINK

## Philosophy
Reviews are the feedback loops that turn experience into growth. Without structured review, execution becomes busywork. This system makes review feel natural, not burdensome.

## Review Hierarchy

```
Daily Review (10 min)
↓
Weekly Review (45 min)
↓
Monthly Review (1–2 hours)
↓
Quarterly Review (half day)
↓
Annual Review (full day)
```

## Daily Review
**When**: Evening (user-configured time)
**Duration**: 10–15 minutes
**Trigger**: App notification at configured time

### Template
1. What did I complete today?
2. What did I skip or defer? Why?
3. How was my energy and focus?
4. What one thing made today meaningful?
5. Tomorrow's top 3 time blocks?

### Output
- Completion log updated
- Tomorrow's schedule previewed
- Mood/energy data point captured

## Weekly Review
**When**: Friday evening or Sunday morning
**Duration**: 30–60 minutes

### Template
1. **Wins**: What went well this week?
2. **Challenges**: What was difficult or blocked?
3. **Learnings**: What did I learn?
4. **Goal progress**: Update each active goal (0–100%)
5. **Domain check-in**: Which domains got attention? Which were neglected?
6. **Energy pattern**: What drained me? What energized me?
7. **Next week intentions**: 3 outcomes I want to achieve
8. **Time blocks for next week**: Schedule them now

### AI Assistance
- AI generates 3 additional personalized questions based on patterns
- AI summarizes the week based on execution data
- AI flags: execution debt, neglected domains, goal drift

## Monthly Review
**When**: Last day of month or first day of new month
**Duration**: 60–90 minutes

### Template
1. Progress on each active goal (detailed)
2. Domain health assessment (all 12)
3. Financial snapshot (if financial domain active)
4. Top 3 wins of the month
5. Top 3 challenges and lessons
6. Habits/rituals: which held, which broke?
7. Next month's strategic priorities
8. One thing to start, stop, continue

## Quarterly Review
**When**: Q1: April 1 / Q2: July 1 / Q3: October 1 / Q4: January 1
**Duration**: 3–4 hours

### Template
1. Life domains balance wheel assessment
2. Goal review: on track / off track / completed / abandoned
3. Major decisions made this quarter
4. Character and values alignment check
5. Relationships: investments made
6. Top 10 lessons of the quarter
7. Next quarter's 3 strategic bets
8. Goals to add, pause, or remove

## Annual Review
**When**: December 31 / January 1
**Duration**: Full day

### Template
1. Year in review narrative (free-form)
2. All domains: deep assessment
3. All goals: final status
4. Major milestones achieved
5. Top 20 lessons of the year
6. Who I became this year (character evolution)
7. What I'm letting go of
8. Vision for next year
9. 3-year view: where am I heading?

## Review UX
- **Guided flow**: step-by-step wizard, can save and resume
- **Pre-populated data**: app surfaces relevant stats automatically
- **AI context**: AI provides summaries to reduce review overhead
- **Progress save**: reviews auto-save as you go
- **Archive**: all reviews stored and searchable
- **PDF export**: generate printable review document

## Review Reminders
- Gentle notification (not aggressive)
- Configurable: disable per review type
- Snooze available (1 day / 1 week)
- No guilt UI — review when ready

## Review Analytics
- Review completion rate (tracks self-discipline)
- Sentiment trends across reviews
- Goal progress velocity
- Domain attention patterns over time
